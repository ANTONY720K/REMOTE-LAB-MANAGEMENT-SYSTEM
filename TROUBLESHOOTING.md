# Troubleshooting Guide

## 1. Connection Issues
   - **Problem:** Unable to connect to the remote lab.
     - **Solution:** Check your internet connection and ensure that the remote lab server is up and running.

## 2. Authentication Errors
   - **Problem:** Login credentials not accepted.
     - **Solution:** Verify your username and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" feature.

## 3. Resource Access Denied
   - **Problem:** Cannot access specific resources or labs.
     - **Solution:** Ensure you have the necessary permissions. Contact your administrator if needed.

## 4. System Errors
   - **Problem:** Encountering system errors or crashes during operation.
     - **Solution:** Restart the application and check for any updates. If the issue persists, report it to the support team with error details.

## 5. Performance Issues
   - **Problem:** Slow response times or lag during operation.
     - **Solution:** Check your network speed. Optimize your connection or try accessing during off-peak hours.

## 6. Feedback & Support
   - If you continue to experience issues, please reach out to the support team through [support link] or create an issue in this repository.
